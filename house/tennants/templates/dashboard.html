About the Dashboard

What is this page?

Your dashboard gives you a real-time overview of your entire property portfolio at a glance.

What you'll find:

  • Stat Cards: Buildings, houses, occupancy and tenant counts
  • Quick Actions: One-click access to common workflows
  • Reports: Generate, filter, export, and print financial & operational reports
  • Recent Activity: Payments, payment requests, issues, charges

Getting Started:

  1. Add a building first
  2. Add houses to that building
  3. Assign tenants to houses
  4. Create monthly rent charges
  5. Record payments as they come in
